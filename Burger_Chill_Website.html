Storage Instructions

Refrigeration: If not consumed immediately, store the burger in an airtight container in the fridge. It can last up to 3 days.

Freezing: For longer storage, wrap the burger tightly in aluminum foil or plastic wrap, then freeze. It can last up to 1 month in the freezer.
